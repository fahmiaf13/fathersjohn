About Father's John
Father's John is a coffee shop that was founded in 2022 by two friends, namely Fahmi and Joni. The name Father's Jon itself is based on Joni's own nickname, Jon. This coffee shop has a franchise concept where these coffee outlets can be found on the side of the road. It is based on people who want the convenience of enjoying coffee without having to wait or visit a coffee shop. So that it can increase mobility for people who are living in big cities with high mobility
